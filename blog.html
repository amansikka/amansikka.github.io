OpenELM: An Efficient Language Model Family with Open-source Training and Inference Framework
An article on OpenELM by Apple, a new open-source LLM family that achieves SOTA performance. It uses a layer-wise scaling to efficiently allocate parameters within each transformer layer.
Anthropic releases research on "mapping the mind" of an LLM
Researchers at Anthropic have scaled sparse autoencoders to SOTA models in order to extract high quality features about the Claude 3 Sonnet model. They have shed a light on the black box of LLMs by creating Interpretable Features from the LLM.
Multi-Token Prediction for Better and Faster LLM
Meta's new groundbreaking paper "Better & Faster Large Language Models via Multi-token Prediction" suggests that training language models to predict multiple future tokens at once results in higher sample efficiency.
